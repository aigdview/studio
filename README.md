Please follow the on-screen instructions:

1. Choose your role: Select either "I am the Candidate" or "I am the Interviewer".
2. Review the Job Description: A default description is provided, but you can edit it as needed.
3. Review the Resume: A default "Your Resume *" will appear if you are the candidate, or a "Candidate's Resume *" if you are the interviewer.
4. Click the "Start Mock Interview" button.
5. Begin the interview based on your selected role.
6. You can mute or unmute your microphone at any time by clicking the mic icon.
7. End the interview by clicking the end call icon.
8. Once you end the call, a personalized Interview Feedback Report will be generated based on your role.
